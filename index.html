How to play?

  • The objective of the game is to put the ball in the hole
  • The key is patience & precision
  • The white ball has a rotating green line which indicates the direction of the hit
  • Wait for the direction line to move in the direction you want to hit
  • Move your mouse over the highlighted area around the ball and adjust the direction
  • Click on the mouse now, the longer you click the more power you will add to the hit
  • Avoid the obstacles and try to get the ball in the hole in as few hits as possible
